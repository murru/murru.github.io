Company Profile

Since 1978, the companies affiliated with Building Components Group, Inc. have supplied prefabricated wood trusses, metal trusses, stairs and railings to the Florida construction trade. During those years, our success as innovators, commitment to quality, competitive prices and a proactive approach to service have made us a leader in the industry.

The most modern technology available, as well as manufacturing facilities in Miami and Ft. Myers, give us a large production capability and the ability to meet the most stringent delivery schedules. You are assured of obtaining a product of the highest quality, strength and durability, engineered and manufactured for faster and efficient installation.
